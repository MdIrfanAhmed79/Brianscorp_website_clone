Our Services



Roofing


From Bronx flat roofs to Westchester shingle systems, our team repairs and installs EPDM, Modified Bitumen, architectural shingles, and more. Whether you’re dealing with leaks, storm damage, or aging materials, we deliver durable, long-lasting roofing solutions.



Chimney


We repair and rebuild chimneys affected by cracked mortar, leaning structures, and water intrusion—common issues in older Bronx and Westchester homes. Our team handles flashing, repointing, crown repair, and full restorations.



Fascia


Protect your home from water damage with professional fascia repair and installation. We replace rotted or aging fascia boards, reinforce roof edges, and ensure proper sealing to prevent long-term moisture problems.



Siding


We install and repair vinyl, wood, and fiber-cement siding to improve insulation, curb appeal, and weather protection. Whether restoring older Bronx homes or upgrading Westchester properties, we deliver clean, durable results.



Gutters


Properly installed gutters prevent water intrusion, foundation issues, and roof damage. We repair, replace, and install seamless gutter systems designed to handle heavy New York rain and protect your home year-round.
